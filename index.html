logo

design

A cat resting on a mountain and wave symbolize the client’s connection with nature and animals.

Warm beige and brown tones evoke familiarity, while a green-to-blue gradient reflects their love for nature.

The design employs principles like balance and analogous colour harmony to create a cohesive composition, though I identified opportunities to improve focus through contrast.

Diptych

design

Inspired by Japanese horror films, this two-panel poster explores the fear of being trapped in ominously altered spaces.

A dark hallway and staircase, drenched in haunting green tones, evoke isolation, while vivid red accents add contrast and intensity.

The design balances eerie visuals with narrative flow, employing principles like contrast, hierarchy, and harmony.

This project challenged me to step outside my comfort zone, deepening my understanding of storytelling through design.
