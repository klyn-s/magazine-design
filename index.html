logo

design

This project was an exploration of turning a client’s story into a meaningful visual identity

A cat resting on a mountain and wave symbolize the client’s connection with nature and animals

Warm beige and brown tones evoke familiarity, while a green-to-blue gradient reflects their love for nature

The design employs principles like balance and analogous colour harmony to create a cohesive and impactful composition, though I identified opportunities to improve focus through contrast.
